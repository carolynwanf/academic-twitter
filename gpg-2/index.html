How would you characterize the Climate Science community on Twitter (eg. size, kinds of people, attitudes, dynamics)?


I think the science Twitter community is very generic and boring. Everybody tweets the same, thinks the same, and designs their profiles the same. Very uninspiring. There is a certain norm for how one should behave as a mainstream "academic tweeter" to fit in, and I can't recall seeing many people who break awat from these templates. It's hard to totally explain, but you see the patterns once you've seen enough. Typical posts: "Check out this new paper by me and colleagues! Super cool!" I think a lot of these are legitimate social issues, but I just think these tweets are very superficial and not helping solve any problems, more about fitting in.
